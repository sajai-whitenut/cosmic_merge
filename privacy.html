Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Cosmic Merge ("the Game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

1. Identity & Integrity

Cosmic Merge is designed as a standalone experience. We do not collect, process, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require a user account, email address, or any personal details to play.

2. Data Sovereignty

The Game uses standard browser/device Local Storage solely to save your local High Score on your device. This data is private, stays on your hardware, and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Static Environment

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, cookies, or advertising SDKs that collect user data. The game binary is static and focused entirely on the gameplay mechanics.

4. Age Appropriateness

Since the Game does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages. We comply with child online privacy protection standards by simply not gathering any data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. This policy was finalized on April 27, 2026.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the feedback section in the Google Play Store.
